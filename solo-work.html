Solo Work

Here is a collection of all my musical work not affiliated with anything else, just done for fun and personal improvement.

The Deep Sea Funk, Tango and Dance

This is a track of me playing with the "Jaws" theme and taking it into unexpected places musically.

It was kind of an exercise in writing vastly different genres, as noted by the title, it starts as Jaws, then switches to a chiptune-funk, back to Jaws, and into an uptempo tango or milonga style, and then closes out with a more electronic pumping dance sound.

I submitted this to a Battle of The Bits competition about Sea Monsters, and won second place!

Slice & Dice

A fun chiptune, latin jazz track I did in pxTone Collage.

I also submitted this one to Battle of The Bits. Every summer they have a huge "Summer Chip" competition that lasts months that gets hundreds of entries.

This track won first place among entries in the pxTone category, and 17th overall.

The Three Toed Amigos

This track was originally for a friends' game about a cat fighting a bunch of birds. Each bird would give you a power that you could use against the others like in Megaman.
The game never ended up being finished, however.

Each bird was based on different species from around the world. This was the theme for the toucan from Central/South America. The music has a lot of Latin Samba rhythms but uses Spanish Flamenco harmonies to create a more serious mood.

This track was created in Famitracker with the VRC6 and MMC5 expansion chips.

Catwalk

A relaxed map theme for my friend's unfinished game mentioned above.
